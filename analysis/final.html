Note, for both models, we fit using centered predictors. Doesn't make a difference to coefficient results, but by making the intercept 0, we get easier to interpret marginal plots later on.

broadDataExtinction <- broadData[which(!is.na(broadData$BC.extinction.ratePBDB)),]
